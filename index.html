About

Per Ardua Ad Astra - Through adversity to the stars

In the fast-evolving landscape of African innovation, Tee lives at the forefront, seamlessly navigating the intersections of fintech, real estate, and agribusiness. With first-class degrees in Innovation & Technology Management, Omotade has dedicated his career to closing market gaps, optimizing value chains, and driving sustainable economic growth across the African continent.

As a product manager and engineer, he has played a pivotal role in advancing financial inclusion, spearheading the development of global payroll consumer banking apps, payment APIs, and asset financing solutions.

Beyond fintech, his work spans the fields of real estate and agribusiness. His ability to blend technical expertise with a deep understanding of market dynamics has made him a driving force in Africa’s digital transformation. As he continues to shape industries through innovation, his work is redefining how technology can unlock economic opportunities across the continent.

Beginnings

Anthony Omotade Faweya’s journey has been shaped by a deep passion for technology, leadership, and problem-solving. Excelling academically, he earned a degree in Information Technology and an MSc in Technology and Innovation Management from the University of Sussex. His early leadership roles laid the foundation for his ability to drive innovation and execute impactful solutions.


The Present: Building the Next Generation of Solutions

With experience in high-growth fintech startups across Africa and beyond, Omotade has built and scaled B2B and B2C products that enhance financial accessibility. At Asaak, a $30 million asset financing company, he has spearheaded AI-driven financial solutions, improved digital lending processes, and driven significant customer growth. His work in Global Payroll at Remote streamlined complex financial operations, reducing processing time and improving automation. Prior to this, he contributed to ExxonMobil, Paga, and IITA, gaining valuable experience in enterprise systems and process optimization.

Committed to driving innovation at Aleph., Omotade is focused on building AI-driven, scalable products that empower businesses and individuals in the government, agriculture and payment industries. With a strong foundation in technology and strategy, he continues to push the boundaries of innovation, ensuring products are not just built, but built right.
